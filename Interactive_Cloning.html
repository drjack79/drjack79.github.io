5. Final Verification

Excellent! You've identified the correct colonies and interpreted the controls. The final step is to verify the insert's presence and orientation.

A) Restriction Digest Analysis

Isolating the plasmid and digesting with EcoRI & HindIII is one way to verify the insert. A successful clone will yield two bands on a gel: the vector backbone (~2686 bp) and your `geneX` insert (750 bp).
